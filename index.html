A City Drowning in Single-Use Waste

New York's vibrant food scene comes at a cost. The linear "take-make-dispose" model is unsustainable for our city and our planet.

Millions

Of single-use containers end up in landfills daily, straining our waste management systems.

Tons

Of plastic pollution threatens our local waterways and ecosystems every year.

Rising Costs

For restaurants facing increasing prices for disposable packaging and waste removal.

Our Solution: The Bolla Ecosystem

We've created a seamless, circular system that benefits everyone. This is how we transform a single meal into a collective impact.

1. Purchase

Consumer Engagement

Customers buy a high-quality, reusable Bolla silicone container at a partner restaurant, starting their journey.

2. Reward

Eco-Reward Program

Using the Bolla app, every reuse earns "Green Points" for discounts, eco-products, or non-profit donations.

3. Partner

Restaurant Network

Healthy fast-casual restaurants join our network, reducing waste and attracting loyal, conscious consumers.

4. Impact

Non-Profit Backing

We align with leading NYC environmental non-profits, adding credibility and amplifying our collective mission.
